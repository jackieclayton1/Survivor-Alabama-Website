Survivor: Alabama

Do you have what it takes to be the sole Survivor?

About the Event

Survivor Alabama is a modified version of CBS 'Survivor' where students compete to win the title of Sole Survivor. All season footage will either be provided by our production crew or castaways themselves.

This will be hosted over the Fall 2025 semester, beginning in August and concluding in November. We're looking for University of Alabama students from all walks of life to play in the inaugural season.

What to Expect

Outwit, Outplay, Outlast

  • Engaging Challenges: These unique challenges are designed to test a variety of skills!
  • Exciting Twists: Adapted to the college Survivor format, these twists are sure to shake up the game!
  • Friends For a Lifetime: Every castaway will leave the game with tight bonds that only Survivor could forge!

Format: Merge

Immunity Challenge

Castaways compete for individual immunity, which means they will be safe going into the next tribal council.

Down Time

The time in between a challenge and tribal council is where the magic happens. Castaways have time to socialize and more importantly, strategize in preparation for the upcoming tribal council.

Tribal Council

Tribal council is where the merged tribe cast their votes to eliminate someone from the game and send them to the jury.
